Lasagna

A lovely lasagna dinner that will leave your taste buds in awe

One of Chicago's favourite dishes

Ingredients

  • Sweet italian sausage and lean ground beef
  • An onion and two cloves of garlic
  • Can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese

Recipe Intructions

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
